Top three activities to do at Shymkent

Attend Altyn eye that is located in south of Kazakhstan,

It is the biggest ferris wheel in Kazakstan. Height is 50 metres. Altyn Eye, which means "Golden Eye of Shymkent" in Anglo-Kazakh.

Go to shopping mall named Shymkent Plaza.

It is located in a prestigious area of the city, whose population has a high income. On weekdays, the shopping center is visited by 10-15 thousand people a day.

Want to try to eat a popular street food? Try tandoor samsa.

The samsa is often baked in the tandoor. It is a is a special clay oven and you glue the fresh samsa on the surface inside of the tandoor.
